Eco-Shirt

Our wide range of t-shirts are made from recycled cotton and jute fiber. It is soft and slim fit and, most importantly, it wicks sweat faster. We have 2 types of t-shirts; HeatGear and ColdGear.

HeatGear: Its a game-changing fabric that absorbs sweat from your skin and then speeds it to the surface where it can evaporate faster. Way faster than ordinary cotton.

ColdGrear: This line of products dissipate and re-circulate heat around the wearer's body using ceramic powder. This was purportedly designed so that the wearer's heat signature will not be picked up. It also has scent control technology. This line is designed so that the wearer's scent cannot be detected.
